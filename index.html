Cybersecurity and Information Security

Cybersecurity and Information Security are essential in today's digital era. Cybersecurity involves protecting systems, networks, and data from cyberattacks that aim to steal or damage sensitive information or disrupt business operations. On the other hand, Information Security focuses on safeguarding data in all forms, ensuring its confidentiality, integrity, and availability. Both are crucial for preventing unauthorized access, maintaining trust, and ensuring regulatory compliance. Best practices include using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, updating systems regularly, educating users about phishing, and backing up critical data. Together, these measures create a secure environment for digital and physical information.
